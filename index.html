What Mittar Offers

Everything you need from a real friend

Designed with the Punjabi community at heart — warm, familiar, and never clinical.

💬

Daily Check-ins

Every morning, Mittar reaches out first. A simple "Sat Sri Akal — how are you today?" so no day starts in silence. Over time, it learns your rhythms and adjusts to how you like to talk.

🧠

Culturally Aware AI

Mittar understands the Punjabi experience — the pride, the distance, the in-between. It doesn't need things explained. It already knows what it means to be far from home but never far from your roots.

🌍

Full Punjabi Mode

Switch the entire app to Gurmukhi script with one tap. Mittar responds fully in Punjabi — not translated, but naturally written — from casual chat to deeper conversations.

🔥

Streak Tracking

Your daily streak is a record of every day you showed up for yourself. Miss a day and Mittar checks in. Keep going and it celebrates with you.

😊

Mood Check-ins

Tell Mittar how you're feeling — Happy, Okay, Low, Stressed, or Tired — and the whole conversation shifts to match. On hard days it listens more. On good days it celebrates with you.

🔒

Honest & Private

Mittar will always tell you it's an AI. Your conversations are yours alone. No ads, no data selling, no corporate agenda. Just a safe space to be yourself.

🎙️

Voice Messages

Send Mittar a voice note in English or Punjabi and it responds in kind — making conversations feel less like texting and more like actually talking to a friend.

📖

Memory & Context

Mittar remembers. Your family names, your upcoming events, the things weighing on you. Every conversation picks up where the last one left off.

🌙

Evening Reflections

At the end of the day, Mittar checks back in. A gentle question to help you close out the day — what went well, what's on your mind, what you're grateful for.

Bilingual by Heart

Punjabi isn't just a language toggle

When you switch to Punjabi, everything transforms — Gurmukhi script, Punjabi notifications, and an AI that thinks in your mother tongue. Not translated. Actually Punjabi.

Who It's For

Built for our community

For every Punjabi who's ever felt far from home — wherever in the world they are.

✈️

Diaspora Families

Settled in Canada, UK, USA, or Australia — close to their adopted home but never quite done missing Punjab. Mittar keeps culture alive across the distance.

🎓

International Students

Arriving in a new country, navigating homesickness alongside lectures and deadlines. Mittar is the familiar voice that makes a foreign city feel less foreign.

👨‍👩‍👧

Second Generation

Born abroad, raised between two worlds. Mittar helps second-generation Punjabis explore their heritage, practise the language, and feel connected to a culture that's always been theirs.

👴

Elders & Seniors

Living far from extended family, most comfortable in Punjabi. Mittar meets them where they are — patient, warm, and fully fluent in their language.

💼

Professionals Abroad

Building careers in new cities, often alone. Mittar is the check-in at the end of a long day — no small talk required, just someone who gets it.

🌱

Language Learners

Want to reconnect with Punjabi or learn it for the first time? Mittar is a patient, judgment-free companion for anyone wanting to reclaim their mother tongue.
